Solution

This portion of the website redesign process focuses on the Call-to-Action and Style Tile.

A style tile is used to distill down the main aspects of a website, without desiging a full mock up. It includes all of the necessary aspects, from logo, color palette, typography, and graaphis to the overal feel of the finished website.

For my Call to Action, I chose simplicity and went with “Meet Our Animals”. When someone clicks on the “Meet Our Aniamls” button, they will be directed to a page that has all of the animals that are available for adoption. There will also be information about how to notify PAWS of a lost animal or if one of the animals on the site is your lost pet. Due to the lost/found aspect of PAWS’ mission, a Call to Action addressing “adoptable” or “available” animals seemed inadequate. The phrase "Meet Our Animals" is much more inviting and low risk than "View Adoptable Animals"
