The Design Challenge

This Design Challenge template was created by IDEO. Below are the questions posed by IDEO and my answers.

What is the problem you’re trying to solve?

The website for PAWS Anderson County Animal Shelter is not sufficient in providing information or reaching its audience.

Frame it as a design question.

How can the website reach it's audience and provide them with important information easily?

Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

Help PAWS reach it's correct audience in order to increase adoptions, donations, volunteers, foster opportunities, and improve the overal quality of the shelter.

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Creating a strong brand identity to set PAWS apart and give it's audience a clear vision.

Creating a separate website for PAWS, apart from the Anderson County site.

Providing on-brand graphics and media content that clearly defines information, events, and ways to get involved.

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

The animal shelter seems to be run by the county, so they probably don't have a huge media budget or anything. Only services the Anderson/Upstate area. Basically run on volunteers, which can lead to inconsisitent service and information.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can I design a website and brand identity for PAWS Anderson County Animal SHelter, that will assist them in reaching potential volunteers, adoptors, funding for the shelter, and enevitably help provide animals a safe and loving home?
